
    GlobalCorp 2025 Sustainability and ESG Impact Report.
    
    A Message from the CEO:
    This year, GlobalCorp has made unprecedented strides in our commitment to a greener future. 
    Our carbon footprint has been reduced by 15% across our global supply chain. We are committed 
    to diversity, equity, and inclusion across all our corporate boards.
    
    Environmental Initiatives:
    To offset our remaining carbon emissions, we have invested heavily in global reforestation.
    Specifically, in March 2023, we successfully planted over 50,000 native trees in the 
    Mato Grosso region of Brazil (Coordinates: -12.97, -55.99). We expect this to capture 
    10,000 tons of CO2 over the next decade.
    
    Social Governance:
    We have increased our philanthropic giving by 20% and instituted a new remote work policy 
    to improve employee well-being.
    
    Water Conservation:
    Our manufacturing plants in Southeast Asia have reduced water consumption by implementing 
    new closed-loop cooling systems. Furthermore, we restored 500 acres of wetlands near 
    our Jakarta facility (Coordinates: -6.20, 106.81) in late 2024.
